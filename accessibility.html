Trust and Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Making learning easier to perceive, navigate, and understand

Last updated: 15 July 2026. Athanas Inspires is committed to improving access for learners using different devices, connection speeds, keyboards, assistive technologies, and motion preferences.

Current accessibility features

  • Skip-to-content navigation and semantic page landmarks.
  • Keyboard-visible focus indicators and accessible navigation controls.
  • Strong text contrast improvements across light and dark sections.
  • Reduced-motion support for visitors who prefer fewer animations.
  • Manual play and pause controls for welcome audio and testimonials.
  • Written transcripts for welcome audio messages.
  • Alternative text for meaningful website images.
  • Responsive layouts for phones, tablets, and computers.

Known limitations

Some third-party YouTube, WhatsApp, or embedded content may have accessibility behaviour controlled by the external provider. Older website sections may still require continued refinement.

Request assistance

If a page, lesson, file, or tool is difficult to use, contact Athanas Inspires through the contact page. Please describe the page, device, browser, and difficulty so the issue can be investigated.
