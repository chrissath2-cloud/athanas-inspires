What is Athanas Inspires?

Athanas Inspires is a growing learning and impact platform that helps people learn, believe, grow, and build.

It brings together beginner-friendly ICT lessons, practical assignments, digital tools, Tanzanian education resources, faith-rooted inspiration, YouTube learning, and a supportive WhatsApp community. Everything is designed to help people move from confusion to clarity, build confidence, and grow with purpose.

Learn More About Us →
Are the ICT lessons free?

Yes. The ICT lessons currently available through Athanas Inspires are free.

You can watch the beginner-friendly lessons on YouTube, follow the learning series through the website, practise with available assignments, and use the free interactive learning tools. New lessons and resources will continue to be added as the platform grows.

Explore Free ICT Lessons →
How can I join the WhatsApp ICT community?

You can join the Athanas Inspires ICT community through the official WhatsApp invitation link available on the website.

The community connects learners from different countries who are developing practical ICT skills. Members receive lesson updates, assignment reminders, learning support, encouragement, and opportunities to share their progress.
