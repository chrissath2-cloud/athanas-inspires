Typing Growth Path

  • First goal: type without fear.
  • Second goal: improve accuracy.
  • Third goal: increase speed little by little.
  • Final goal: use typing to learn, work, and create faster.

Practice Tips

  • Keep both hands on the keyboard.
  • Look at the screen more than your fingers.
  • Do not rush the first round.
  • Repeat the same level until your accuracy improves.
